Privacy Policy

Fandom Quiz — Last updated: April 10, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Fandom Quiz ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play.

Fandom Quiz does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not require account creation, login, or any personal information to use the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address books
  • Photos, files, or media
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Device identifiers for our own analytics

Locally Stored Data

The App stores your game progress, settings, and quiz scores locally on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device advertising identifier (GAID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings or visiting Google Ads Settings.

AI-Generated Content

Quiz questions are generated using artificial intelligence services. The requests sent to generate questions contain only the name of the selected show or movie and game parameters. No personal data is included in these requests.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App does not collect personal data from any user, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on any server, there is minimal risk of a data breach from our side. All game data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's own security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

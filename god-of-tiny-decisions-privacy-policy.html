Overview

God of Tiny Decisions is a single-player card game. We respect your privacy and designed the app to collect as little data as possible.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No user accounts or registration required.
  • No analytics or tracking services.
  • No access to contacts, camera, location, or microphone.

Local Storage

The app stores game progress, statistics, and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Advertising

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. We request non-personalized ads only, which means AdMob does not use your personal data for ad targeting.

However, Google may still collect certain technical data (such as device identifiers and IP address) as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

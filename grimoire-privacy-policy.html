Privacy Policy

Grimoire – Spell the Dark Away

Last updated: February 2026

Grimoire (“the Game”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the game and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Grimoire does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information directly. However, the following data may be processed:

a) Gameplay Data (Stored Locally)

  • Campaign progress
  • Stars earned
  • XP and statistics
  • Rune inventory
  • Language preference

This data is stored locally on your device using secure storage and is not transmitted to us.

b) Advertising Data

Grimoire uses third-party advertising services provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID
  • Interaction data with ads

This data is used to provide and personalize advertisements. For more information, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Children’s Privacy

Grimoire is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer personal data. Advertising providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Security

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain external databases of user data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
